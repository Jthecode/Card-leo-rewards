1. Overview

Card Leo Rewards uses cookies and related technologies to help the website function, support secure authentication, remember certain state during portal activity, and improve the reliability of the member experience.

Some cookies are necessary for basic platform operations, especially where login, account verification, password recovery, portal access, or support features are involved.

Because Card Leo Rewards includes a protected member platform, certain cookies and session tools are essential to secure access and cannot be fully disabled without affecting functionality.

2. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files placed on your browser or device when you visit a website. They can be used to remember information about your visit, maintain a login session, support form flows, or help a site understand how it is being used.

Similar technologies may include browser storage, temporary session identifiers, authentication tokens, or security-related state used by web applications.

3. How We Use Them

Card Leo Rewards may use cookies and related technologies to:

  • keep you signed in to the member portal when authorized;
  • maintain secure session state across protected pages;
  • support login, logout, password reset, and email verification flows;
  • remember temporary preferences or settings tied to your session;
  • help protect against abuse, unauthorized access, or suspicious behavior;
  • improve platform stability, troubleshooting, and operational support; and
  • ensure that public pages and member workflows function as intended.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential cookies

These are necessary for core site and portal functionality, including session continuity, protected route access, login state, and account security.

Authentication cookies

These help identify whether you are signed in and whether your current browser session should be allowed to access protected member pages.

Operational cookies

These may help with short-term workflow continuity, such as holding state during portal use, settings changes, or support submissions.

Security-related cookies

These help support account integrity, session tracking, password resets, verification flows, and detection of unusual access patterns.

Card Leo Rewards does not describe cookies in this policy by requiring technical knowledge. The main purpose is to explain how these tools support real platform functionality and secure member access.

5. Session & Security Technology

Card Leo Rewards uses secure session handling in connection with the member portal. This may include browser cookies and session records used to support:

  • authenticated portal access;
  • member-only profile, rewards, settings, and support pages;
  • active session management across devices or browsers;
  • sign-out controls and session invalidation;
  • password update and account recovery processes; and
  • verification that requests are coming from a valid signed-in context.

These technologies may also help us respond to suspicious activity, limit abuse, and maintain the overall security posture of the platform.

6. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to control or delete cookies through browser settings. Depending on your browser, you may be able to:

  • view cookies stored on your device;
  • delete individual cookies or all cookies;
  • block future cookie placement; or
  • configure preferences for particular websites.

Because browser controls vary, you should review your browser’s help or settings documentation for instructions specific to your device or browser version.

7. What Happens If You Disable Them

If you disable essential cookies or session-related storage, some or all of the following may stop working correctly:

  • member login and logout behavior;
  • protected portal page access;
  • session continuity across portal pages;
  • password reset and email verification workflows;
  • settings updates and account-related actions; and
  • support requests submitted through authenticated portal flows.

In short, disabling required cookies may prevent you from using core parts of the Card Leo Rewards member platform.

8. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the Card Leo Rewards platform, authentication flow, infrastructure, security practices, or legal obligations.

When we make updates, we may revise the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the website or portal after updated terms are posted means the revised policy may apply going forward.
